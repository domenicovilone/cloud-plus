This website is for individuals and small companies looking to store files, images and many more.

The clients expectations:

  1. Easy to use Website
  2. Modern Design
  3. Intuitive Design

Competitors:

Dropbox

Cons

- Dropbox site has a counter-intuitive layout, which is found to be more challenging to navigate than the other competitors.

- Most popular out of all competitors

Pros

- Synchronize with multiple devices

- Dedicated support team.
Google Drive

Cons

- Makes it difficult to integrate with other forms of communication.

- Limited in the number of plugins that can be used within it.

Pros

- Much easier to manage. There are no complexities associated with maneuvering through the documents.

- Easier to share files with other users than it is with other storage platforms.
iCloud

Cons

- There has been several hacks to iCloud so security is always a concern..

- There is no web interface for photos or music.

Pros

- The ability to share any type of information easily and without any type of additional hardware.

- With iCloud backup, no document is going to get lost. If something is deleted from a device, you can retrieve it from iCloud.
